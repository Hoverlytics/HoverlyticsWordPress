Hoverlytics lets you see your Google Analytics stats in seconds, in a neat panel directly on your website.

== Description ==

With Hoverlytics installed, simply hover your mouse over any post or page, and Hoverlytics presents the latest info from Google Analytics in a neat minimizable panel.

Hover from post to post and compare monthly visitors, top sources, new vs returning visits, total page views, average time on page, and more.

== Installation ==

To install the Hoverlytics plugin and get it running:

1. In the WordPress dashboard, go to Plugins in the menu on the left. Select **Add New**. Choose **Upload**.
2. Select the `hoverlytics-plugin.zip` you have recently downloaded, and upload it.
3. Click **Activate** under the Hoverlytics item.
4. Go to your website's homepage. The Hoverlytics panel should be in the bottom left of your website. *Note that only you as an administrator can only see the Hoverlytics panel.*
5. In another tab, log on to [http://www.google.com/analytics/](http://www.google.com/analytics/). The Hoverlytics panel has a link to Google Analytics you can click on.
6. Back on your website, click Authorize.
7. Choose the Analytics profile for your website from the list.
8. Hoverlytics is now ready. Hover over any link on your website to see its stats appear in front of your eyes. Enjoy.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where does Hoverlytics load its stats from? =

Hoverlytics relies on Google Analytics, so you must have the Google Analytics snippet installed on your website.
